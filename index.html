Double Majors, Double Degrees and Pre-Med Programs

We encourage students to pursue two majors or pre-med, which are possible to complete within a reasonable time schedule. Graduates with two majors have more job options and they stand out compared with their single-major peers. These graduates are in greater demand as new technologies require multi-disciplinary knowledge. With broader knowledge, they will be more creative and will be able to adapt more easily to changes in the market. Here are sample programs of several popular combinations
